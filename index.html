Plant a forest from your couch
You can sponsor a tree in two minutes: we'll plant it and you'll get to watch it grow through real photos every season. No pot, no mud, no excuses.
How to turn a click into a forest
Choose where you want to leave your mark: a burned-out forest, a school, or a neighborhood square. You decide the destination.
Local teams plant your tree using native species. No quick-grow monocultures.
You'll receive geotagged photos every season. Your tree has a name, a location, and a story that begins with you.
Every tree cleans the air, prevents erosion, and provides jobs for rural communities. Plant one, and you make a difference in three ways.
